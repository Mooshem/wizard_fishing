General Game Overview:

The game is about progressing trades as a wizard in a world of magic. 

Trades:

1. Fisher
2. Vulcan
3. Brew-Master
4. Botanist
5. Webster
6. Sorcerer (combat and killing focused)

Lore:

In this world, each town has an appointed wizard. The wizard of this town has dissapeared, and the town has suffured an attack
by a dragon. Your job is to step in and help rebuild the town, and become the new appointed wizard. Discover the secret of the 
missing wizard.
